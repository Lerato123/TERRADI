About Us

Innovative Filmmaking

TEERADI is an audio-drama/ film production company that produces films from written works of art into high-quality audio-drama content or films. Founded in 2023, with the passion to leave behind indigenous stories of Africa’s heritage, with a mix, in such a way that they echo on into the future, forever in the back of one’s mind. Films that will evoke a sense of nostalgia and reminiscence whenever they are remembered. We specialize in two categories which are audio-drama accompanied with static visual elements such as book cover art; and animation films. Our team of talented artists and writers brings together diverse backgrounds to create unique and engaging experiences for our audience.

Aim

TEERADI aims to empower emerging African writers, voice artists, and sound designers, creating a collaborative platform where fresh talent and experienced creators come together to shape authentic audio experiences rooted in African heritage and contemporary realities

Vision

To be the leading storyteller of African voices and experiences through immersive audio-dramas and animation that bridge diverse cultures, traditions, and modern narratives, bringing the heart of South Africa’s stories to a global stage.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to craft high-quality, innovative audio-dramas and animation that connect with listeners on an emotional level and highlight the richness of African stories, giving voice to local communities and connecting audiences worldwide. We are dedicated to providing content that is culturally insightful, emotionally echoing, and universally appealing, fostering a sense of pride, unity, and appreciation for African narratives.
